5 Weekly Habits

1
Friday: 5-Sentence Status Email
Every active client. What shipped, what's coming, what you need. Same format weekly.
2
Before Calls: One Curious Question
About their business, not the project. Write it down. This becomes your intelligence.
3
Before Meetings: Define the Outcome
"By end of this call, client will…" State it at the top. Call it at the close.
4
Within 2 Hrs: Post-Meeting Recap
Decided / doing next (owner + date) / need from you. 5-8 lines. Copy John on big ones.
5
Monthly: Send Something They Didn't Ask For
Article, competitor move, data point. Two sentences of context. No ask. Builds trust.
